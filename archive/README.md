# Archive

This folder stores raw notes, source references, and historical receipts that belong in the record but not in polished publishable surfaces.

## Purpose

The archive is the basement. It holds things that matter but are not ready for public consumption — or things that were once working materials and have since been superseded.

This includes:
- Raw brainstorm notes and early-stage thinking
- Source links, references, and research inputs
- Changelog and version history
- Anything that documents decisions made during the build without being presentable to a general audience

## Files

- `raw-notes.md` — Unfiltered working notes and scratch thinking
- `source-links.md` — References, articles, tools, and resources that informed the project
- `changelog.md` — Version history: what changed, when, and why

## Principle

Do not delete things. Move them here instead. The archive is for preservation, not for hiding. If something needs to be removed entirely, note that in the changelog.
