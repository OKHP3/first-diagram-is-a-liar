## Ranking Criteria

- **Clarity** — Does the diagram communicate the intended idea without prior context?
- **Accuracy** — Does it correctly represent the system or argument?
- **ROY** — Does it produce more understanding than the words alone?
- **Visual Logic** — Are the structural choices (layout, arrows, hierarchy) intentional?
