# Bundle Notes

Ideas for Ko-fi supporter bundles tied to the article and related materials.

---

## Possible Bundles

### Diagram Bundle
- Full Mermaid source files (V1 and V2 rounds)
- Exported PNGs in high resolution
- Council scorecard summary
- Notes on what changed between rounds and why

### Article + Deck Bundle
- Final v1.0 article (PDF formatted)
- ETCH-AI-SKETCH slide deck (PDF)
- Annotated version with margin notes on key decisions

### Council of AIs Bundle
- Full interview transcripts (all models, all rounds)
- Comparative rankings table
- Synthesis notes: where models agreed, where they diverged, what it means

## Notes

- Bundles should only be assembled when the underlying materials are ready
- Price based on tangible value, not effort
- Do not bundle placeholder content
