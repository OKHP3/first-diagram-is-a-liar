# Membership Ideas

Notes on potential Ko-fi recurring membership tiers for OverKill Hill P³.

---

## What Membership Would Offer

Recurring support in exchange for early access to materials, behind-the-scenes notes, and visibility into the public build process before it goes live.

This is not a community or a forum. It is access to the working layer: drafts before they are polished, diagrams before they are exported, notes from the council process as they happen.

---

## Possible Tiers

### Tier 1: Observer
- Early access to article drafts
- Changelog updates before public posts

### Tier 2: Contributor
- Everything in Observer
- Access to raw council interview notes
- Input on poll question framing (not votes — framing)

### Tier 3: Collaborator
- Everything in Contributor
- Named acknowledgment in article credits
- Direct feedback round on a draft version

---

## Guardrails

- Do not over-promise. Describe exactly what members get.
- Do not create a support burden that slows the public work.
- Revisit this when the public project has real traction.
