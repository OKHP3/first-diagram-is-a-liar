The First Diagram Is Usually a Liar

A public-build article on diagrams, words, AI synthesis, and the cost of getting it wrong the first time.

The first diagram you draw is usually premature. It reflects your assumptions, not the system. ROY — Return on Your Words — measures how much understanding a piece of communication actually produces. This project documents how to get to the diagram that earns its place: through iteration, a Council of AIs, LinkedIn poll rounds, and a public build from v0.1 to v1.0.
